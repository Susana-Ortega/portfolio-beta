Introduction to Research

How is a movie made? What do movie editors do? Editing is one of the most important specialities in fimmaking: We can affirm, without any fear of making a mistake, that it’s the first time we actually see the movie as it going to be in the end. Ir articulates the cinematographical lenguage. but why is it so unknown? Could we make it more atractive for people aproaching this art field? I interviwed around 15 people and this is what I found.

Pain Points

80%

Don’t know what Editing is and what do editors do.

95%

Think it’s a very technical speciality that mainly depends on a software general knowledge.

98%

Don’t know any editor.

The Proposal

  • An article that translates the esence of cinematographical lenguage and the most definibg aspect of editong through graphics.
  • Playing with fragmentation, which is pivotal in filmmaking and seduction.

User Persona: Amalia

Problems
  • Only knows about the main softwares editors use.
  • Almost all the content about it that she finds, aproaches editong mainly from a technical point of view.
  • On the other hand, the contents that speak about the artistic part of this speciality are to complicated and dense.
Needs
  • To understand what every film speciality is all about.
  • To understand how a movie is made.

The Style

Fragmentation is basic in order to articulate cinematographical lenguage. To translate that into graphical lenguage is very important. I also wanted to play with seduction, in the end of the day I want to make editing desirable and interesting.

Colors

I extracted the color palete from movies like In the Mood for Love, Blade Runner, 2047 or Her. All of them approach desire, sensuality from a contemp point of view, the mood I wanted to exhale in this article.
