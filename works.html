Ciclue

When it comes to health, what do women actually need? What does really matter? This app advocates for a different approach to cicle data. Sometimes we oversee and live with symptoms that should be taking care off, just because we are not sure if we should worry or not. But we should receive something in exchange for the data we register in a cicle app and this wxchange should be transparent with users.

El Cine es Cortar

What do audiovisual editors do? This is an approach to this job, in order to make it more atractive for students interested in the film industry. This work tries to translate cinematographical lenguage into graphics and give some answers.
